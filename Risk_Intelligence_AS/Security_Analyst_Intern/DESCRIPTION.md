#### About the job:

If you would like to work on security threat assessments alongside some of the maritime sector’s most respected analysts, look no further. Risk Intelligence is now looking for an intern and would like to hear from you if you are a university student with strong analytical skills and an interest in global threats to maritime and land-based actors.

Risk Intelligence A/S is a recognised leader within security risk intelligence and the company delivers threat and risk assessments globally, powered by specialist risk intelligence analysis and in continuous development to cover emerging threats and trade patterns.

The company’s core product, the Risk Intelligence System, is a digital, intelligence-powered risk monitoring and planning system, that allows clients throughout the end-to-end supply chain to monitor global security risks in integration with their fleet management systems. It is constantly developed in close collaboration with clients and is fully scalable.

#### Risk Intelligence internships:

The purpose of the Risk Intelligence internship is to allow you as an intern to put the theoretical knowledge obtained during your studies into practice – while also gaining professional work experience in the field of security analysis, and insights into organisational practices and procedures. The further aim of the internship is to equip and build upon your theoretical knowledge and strengthen additional skills for future career applications. You will be encouraged to actively engage with and participate in finding solutions to the tasks in the field of activity of the workplace, in order to obtain as much experience as possible.

####  Internship tasks:

As an intern at Risk Intelligence, you will take part in a variety of activities relating to security analysis and research, using OSINT tools. With the support of your supervisor, you will be expected to participate in daily activities in the office, including basic daily tasks, analytical work, and presentations. Your responsibilities will vary and be at different levels of complexity depending on the candidate’s background, skill sets, and interests.

The internship aims to focus, though not exclusively, on assisting with and conducting:

- Conducting independent in-depth research on a specific project linked to client’s needs and current geopolitical issues. This work will result in the drafting and potential publication of a detailed article.
- Assisting analysts in their work: strategic monitoring, OSINT research, specific tools, such as GEOINT or data analysis.
- Providing marketing with writing and editing analysis content.
- End internship presentation.

The specific responsibilities may vary depending on the student’s background and focus. The internship also provides flexibility for the intern to take charge of independent tasks and projects, which can be used to meet university study requirements if need be.

#### Conditions:

The internship is unpaid, and for the autumn/winter 2026-2027 period, with start in September. The workplace will be our offices in Hellerup in the greater Copenhagen area, Denmark. We are open to tailoring the duration of the internship to fit personal requirements, but the overall desired duration of the internship is a minimum of 5 months.
